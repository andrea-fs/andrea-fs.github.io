About Me

Hello! I'm Andrea Fossà, a Master's student in Artificial Intelligence at the University of Bologna, currently an AI Research Intern at Fondazione Bruno Kessler (FBK E3DA Lab). My academic journey includes a BS in Bioinformatics from the University of Verona.


My passion lies in processing bio-signals (EEG) using deep learning techniques on edge devices, with a focus on energy-efficient embedded digital architectures. I've also developed passive Brain-Computer Interfaces (BCI) for assessing Mental Workload. I am always eager to apply my knowledge in Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Computer Vision, and Natural Language Processing to solve complex problems.
